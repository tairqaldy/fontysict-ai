## ğŸ› ï¸ The 4-Step Feedback Process

**Step 1: Giving Effective Feedback**

 **ğŸ“º Watch:** [TED Talk - The Secret to Giving Great Feedback**Links to an external site.**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wtl5UrrgU8c)[![](https://fhict.instructure.com/images/play_overlay.png)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wtl5UrrgU8c)

1. **Micro-yes:** Pace the other person for the feedback you're about to give them
2. **Be specific:** Avoid blur words - give specific feedback about what needs to increase or diminish
3. **Show impact:** Demonstrate the impact of the person's actions
4. **Ask for input:** Request suggestions from the other person to solve the problem

**Step 2: Asking for Feedback**

**âŒ Instead of:** "What do I need to improve?"

**Step 3: Receiving Feedback**

1. **Practice active listening:** Summarize or paraphrase their points to confirm understanding
2. **Avoid defensiveness:** Stay calm and composed during the conversation
3. **Ask clarifying questions:** Ensure you understand the feedback clearly
4. **Request specifics:** If feedback is vague, ask for concrete examples or improvement suggestions
5. **Separate message from messenger:** Focus on the content, not who's delivering it
6. **Take time to reflect:** Process what you've heard before responding or taking action
7. **Follow up:** Check back after reflection and/or implementing changes
