## üéØ Assessment Process

**üí° Assessment Overview:** Teachers will review your final portfolio to understand your semester 2 journey and evaluate your readiness for semester 3. This comprehensive review considers all your work, progress, and learning outcomes achievement.

### Final Delivery Presentation

You'll typically be invited for a final delivery presentation with your teachers. This is your opportunity to showcase your work and discuss your learning journey.

**üìò Important Note:** While the final delivery session isn't mandatory, you always have the right to request it. Even if teachers don't deem it necessary, you can ask for this presentation opportunity.

## üìä Assessment Results

Your semester performance will be summarized with a single assessment grade using the USGO scale:

| Grade | Description    | Requirements & Outcomes                                       |
| ----- | -------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| U     | Unsatisfactory | *Learning outcomes not sufficiently demonstrated*           |
| S     | Satisfactory   | *At least 'Proficient' on all learning outcomes*            |
| G     | Good           | *Strong performance across learning outcomes*               |
| O     | Outstanding    | *Exceptional performance and learning outcomes achievement* |

**üí° Assessment Guarantee:** If you achieve at least 'Proficient' on all learning outcomes, you're guaranteed a 'Satisfactory' grade or better. This allows you to focus on key improvement areas without worrying about meeting minimum requirements.
